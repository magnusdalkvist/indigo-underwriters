About Us

Indigo Underwriters

Indigo Underwriters Ltd (IUL), Indigo Underwriters Danmark ApS (IUD) and Latona S.A. are specialist insurance brokers and subsidiary companies of the Indigo Group.

IUL operates as a Lloyd's coverholder based in the United Kingdom, while IUD, located in the heart of Copenhagen, serves as the European counterpart and is a coverholder for Lloyd's Insurance Company SA. Further we source reinsurance to Bermuda reinsurance carrier Ark. Latona is a Warsaw based Insurance agent for a number of pan European carriers located in Poland.

PhoebeApS

PhoebeApS focuses on investments in the European Insurance Market. With a dedicated team, they finance, acquire and manage insurance businesses within the Indigo Group.

Service

Life, Accident & Health Treaty Reinsurance

We offer these services worldwide. Across the carriers we work with we can offer significant capacities for both per event and per person. More than 60 insurers and reinsurers use Indigo Underwriters for their reinsurance needs.

Service

Trackday and direct insurance

On the direct insurance front, our flagship offering is our market-leading trackday insurance portfolio, available for both four- and two-wheeled vehicles. This service is open to residents across the EU, UK and Switzerland. We are proud to hold a leading position with dedicated local language offerings in the United Kingdom, France, Germany, Denmark, and Sweden.

Other services

In Warsaw, our Latona subsidiary underwrites a portfolio of employee benefit business in addition to our Banc Assurance business. The operations include property, personal and life insurance segments. The Latona team also has experience in implementing group life insurance programs for employees of companies that care about dedicated service processes with minimal staff involvement and employee satisfaction.
